Privacy Policy

Boring explanation about how our privacy works!

We take privacy seriously, so we will explain about our services.

Website Related

I don't track users with Google Analysis or any types of trackers. The website is securely hosted on GitHub, domain provided by Freenom.

Bot Related

I don't store any types of file such as user logs, or any kinds of files.

Other Recommendations

We recommend you to use Mozilla Firefox, it uses less memory compared to Chrome, and they care about privacy a lot, that's why Firefox comes with Enhanced Tracking Protection for all users, which blocks cross-site cookies, fingerprints, cryptominers, social media trackers, etc. Also, you can check if your account details are leaked with Firefox Monitor.

If you shop online, you should install a VPN such as NordVPN (like the one you always see on YouTuber's sponsorship time) so you can shop safely without having your IP being tracked.
